Sample Pipeline Management App

Product design / strategy and user research

Clinical geneticists, variant scientists, and lab directors need a better way to track case flow, conduct fast but accurate tertiary analysis to identify and curate candidate variants, and configure standard operating procedure.

Scope

Clinical Sequence Analyzer (CSA) is a web application used by genetic lab directors, clinical and research geneticists, lab managers, and variant scientists to review an aggregate of variant annotations in the context of a small family's genetic sequences to identify disease-causing candidate variants for an affected member of that family and curate those variants for clinical reports.

Collaboration

I currently lead user research and design efforts for all clinical products, working with the clinical product manager, product owner, our development team, and the multitude of brilliant clinical experts in genomics both within our company and across our customer base.

Outcome

Several projects within CSA are currently underway and are in various phases of the design/development process. One such project is launching our new ACMG curation workflow, which implements the robust guidelines from The American College of Molecular Genetics and Genomics into a streamlined, flexible, and user-validated interface for scoring and classifying variants.
