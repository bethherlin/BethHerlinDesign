Janssen Care Planning Mobile Apps

Research, UX and UI design

Clinical geneticists, variant scientists, and lab directors need a better way to track case flow, conduct fast but accurate tertiary analysis to identify and curate candidate variants, and configure standard operating procedure.

The Client

As the pharmaceutical branch of Johnson & Johnson, Janssen has a vested interest in the adherence to and outcome tracking of its medications.

The Project Scope

Together with one other internal designer and a product management team within Janssen, I launched an exploratory effort investigating the potential for a digital care planning solution to a variety of chronic conditions. This effort included user and literature research and synthesis into the care plans for specific chronic conditions, such as IBD, schizophrenia, diabetes, geriatric, and joint replacement surgery, and the creation of strategic product vision for digital services to facilitate these care plans.

Project Outcome

Among our project deliveries were extensisive illustrated Needs Maps for each condition, prototyped digitial experiences for a schizophrenia care plan management mobile app, and system and information archicture maps.
