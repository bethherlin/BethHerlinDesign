Fracture

Game Design

Two computer science majors and myself sought to create a 2D sidescrolling puzzle platform game unlike any other. We created an elaborate storyline to frame the player as Maxwell, a poor soul unknowingly caught between two very different worlds. He must explore his surroundings through gameplay and various plot reveals to uncover the truth.
