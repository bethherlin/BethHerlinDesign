Genomic News Mobile App

Research, UX and UI design

Clinical geneticists, variant scientists, and lab directors struggle to stay on top of the rapidly changing landscape of genomic understanding, and how those changes affect their clinical casework or research.

Scope

A project was launched to create a mobile solution empowering clinicians and clinical researchers to access and follow genomic research articles of interest from reputable sources, quickly assess the relevance of an article to their work, and obtain an overview of which cases or studies should be reevaluated in the context of identified genes, variants, or phenotypes.

Collaboration

I helped lead user research initiatives to identify and understand the needs of this subset of users, created several iterations of high fidelity clickable wireframes throughout additional user testing, helped carry out the final production UI design and testing with our creative director, and worked with an outsourced team to for development.

Outcome

This mobile iOS and Android application for clinicians and clinical researchers is still in development.
